🔒 Privacy Policy

How we handle your personal information

Your Privacy is Our Priority

At Evently, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us.

Local Storage Only: All event planning data, guest lists, checklist items, budget entries, and sticky notes are stored entirely within your browser's local storage (`localStorage`). We do not upload, sync, or transmit any of your planner data to external servers or databases.

Information Collection: If you use certain features, we only access locally provided details (such as names and notes) to enable application features. We do not track or sell your browsing history.

For any concerns or queries regarding our privacy practices, please contact us.

ℹ️ About Us

Learn more about the Evently team and mission

Our Mission

Evently was created by a passionate group of frontend engineers who believe that planning events should be interactive, simple, and private.

We designed Evently to combine beautiful modern design (glassmorphism, interactive elements, custom light/dark themes) with absolute offline privacy (zero cloud storage, zero tracking).

Whether you're organizing a small birthday dinner or a large corporate meeting, Evently provides you with the guest, budget, task, and reminder tools you need to succeed.
